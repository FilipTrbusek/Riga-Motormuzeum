Riga Motor Museum entrance tickets

Museum's entrance tickets will open doors to the largest and most modern automotive history exposition in the Baltics. Entrance tickets can be purchased on-site at the cashier desk located in the entrance area. Here visitors can not only purchase entrance tickets, but also obtain information regarding guided tours, exhibitions, events, services and other relevant information Visitors have the possibility to buy tickets on-line. Tickets are available at Biļešu Serviss trade points and e-store. Entrance ticket is activated upon entering the exposition. Ticket is valid only on the day it is activated and must be preserved till the end of your visit.
