Riga Motor Museum ranks 3rd on TripAdvisor TOP Things To Do In Riga

Reviews have been provided by thousands of visitors - both foreign tourists and locals. Travelers praise the museum's modern exposition, unique oldtimers, their history, stories, but most of all - that the Riga Motor Museum is suitable not only for car aficionados. A traveller from Greece says:" Riga's Motormuseum is... indeed an interesting place to visit whether you are a kid, a teenager or an adult. There are vehicles ranging from bikes of the 19th century to luxury, racing and everyday cars of the 20th century. There are also points with quizes related to vehicles and traffic and interactive exhibits. If you are to visit, make sure you have at least two hours so that you can fully enjoy it." Positive ratings have granted to the museum a Trip Advisor Certificate of Excellence as well as the Traveler Choice 2017, 2018 and 2019. View TripAdvisor traveller reviews and opinions of Riga Motor Museum.



Riga Motor Museum nominated for the 2018 Historic Motoring Award

The 2018 Historic Motoring Awards Shortlist has been revealed and Riga Motor Museum is one of four finalists in the category “Museum of the Year”. The official award ceremony will take place on October 25 this year in London, and is considered the most prestigious in the industry, recognizing the elite in the international classic... automotive world. Riga Motor Museum is competing for the award with 4 other outstanding museums: Petersen Automotive Museum in the USA, Cité de l'Automobile - musée national collection Schlumpf in France, Museo Nicolis: Auto, Moto, Bici, Aerei E Oggettistica D'epoca in Italy, and Torre de Loizaga-CMV Colección The Miguel de la Vía Museum in Spain, which features one of the largest Rolls-Royce car collections in the world. All finalists can be viewed on The Historic Motoring Award website.
